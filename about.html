Who I Am

I'm jakub, I shoot mostly citiscapes, landscapes and travel photography.

Most of the time I do computer engineering, and this site is my side project.

Approach

The gallery has been created with minimal setup coding philosophy. It doesn't use heavy libraries or assets; just plain html, css and js.

Photos are hosted on Cloudinary and updated regularly.

My Gear

I primarily shoot with a Micro Four-Thirds system of Olympus OM-D E-M5 Mk III paired with a variety of lenses. For cityscapes and architecture, I favor the OM System M. Zuiko Digital 20mm f/1.4 PRO - for its sharpness and edge-to-edge clarity. For landscapes and travel, Olympus M. Zuiko Digital 12-45 f/4 PRO is my go-to.

For film photography, you can see me using mostly two systems - Canon with EOS 300 and Minolta with Dynax 505si Super. Exifs and metadata of my film photos is also visible in the gallery while zooming in on photos.
